Tips & Strategy

🎯 Best Starting Points

Rare letter combos (qx, kz, vz) have better odds. Try prefixes: kz, qx, vw, zx, qk

⚡ Rate Limits

RDAP servers may throttle if you go too fast. If you see errors, switch to Safe — 2s mode.

✅ Verify Before Buying

Always confirm on Namecheap or Cloudflare Registrar before paying.

📋 Save Results

🔤 Brandable Patterns

Aim for CVCV (consonant-vowel-consonant-vowel) patterns like "kelo", "vura" — they sound like real brands.
